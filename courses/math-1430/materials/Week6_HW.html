Calculus 2 • Power Series, Absolute & Conditional Convergence, & Taylor Series

1. Interval of Convergence

Find the interval of convergence of each power series.

  1. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-3)^n}{n} $$
  2. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n!(x-2)^n}{n+1} $$
  3. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n^2(x-5)^n}{n!} $$
  4. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{n(x+2)^n}{3^n} $$
  5. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{4^n x^n}{\sqrt{n+1}} $$
  6. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} 2^n n^2 x^n $$
  7. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} n^n x^n $$
  8. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(x-2)^n}{n^2+1} $$
  9. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n}{2^n(n^2+1)} x^n $$
  10. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-4)^n}{n^3} $$
