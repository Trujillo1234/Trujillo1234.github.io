Calculus 2 • Power Series & Radius of Convergence

1. Calculus on Power Series

Find the integral ($\int f(x) dx$) and derivative ($f'(x)$) of each power series.

  1. $$ f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (-1)^n x^{2n} $$
  2. $$ g(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} n! \, x^n $$
  3. $$ h(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{2^n} $$
  4. $$ f(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} 5^n (x-3)^n $$
  5. $$ g(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(x-5)^{2n}}{n!} $$
  6. $$ h(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} x^{3n} $$

2. New Taylor Series

Find the Taylor Series for each function centered at $x=a$ (using substitution on known series).

  1. $$ \sin(x^3), \quad a=0 $$
  2. $$ \cos(-x), \quad a=0 $$
  3. $$ (x-1)^3, \quad a=0 $$
  4. $$ \frac{1}{1-x^3}, \quad a=0 $$
  5. $$ e^{x^2}, \quad a=0 $$
  6. $$ \frac{1}{1-x/3}, \quad a=0 $$
  7. $$ \frac{\sin(x)}{x}, \quad a=0 $$
  8. $$ \frac{\cos(x)}{x}, \quad a=0 $$
  9. $$ x e^x, \quad a=0 $$
  10. $$ \cos(\sqrt{x}), \quad a=0 $$
  11. $$ \sin(\sqrt{x}), \quad a=0 $$
  12. $$ e^{3x}, \quad a=0 $$
  13. $$ e^{x/2}, \quad a=0 $$

3. Radius of Convergence

Find the center ($a$) and radius of convergence ($R$) for each power series.

  1. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} 2^n x^n $$
  2. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{3^n} $$
  3. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{n x^n}{2^n} $$
  4. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{e^n} $$
  5. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{10^n x^n}{n!} $$
  6. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n x^n}{n!} $$
  7. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} 3^n (x-1)^n $$
  8. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n(x-3)^n}{5^n} $$
  9. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-10)^n}{n!} $$
