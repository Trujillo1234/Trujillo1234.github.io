Section 6 Logarithms and Exponentials

Section Context

This unit completes the course with exponential and logarithmic functions. Students connect growth factors, inverse relationships, graph transformations, logarithm properties, equations, and applied models.

  • Learning objectives: interpret \(ab^x\), use logarithms as inverse operations, transform exponential and logarithmic graphs, solve equations, and model growth or decay.
  • Representative example: solve \(2e^{0.4t}=10\) by isolating the exponential term and applying \(\ln\) to both sides.
  • Connection forward: exponential and logarithmic models are foundational in calculus, differential equations, statistics, and applied modeling.
