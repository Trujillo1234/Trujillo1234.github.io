Research Interests

My research focuses on Ramsey theory, topological Ramsey spaces, ultrafilters, nonstandard models of arithmetic, and related questions in mathematical logic and set theory. I am especially interested in how combinatorial structure can organize infinite mathematical objects and how different notions of reducibility and classification reveal the shape of those objects.
