Homework 9

Calculus 2 • Work & Centroids
  1. A $20$ meter cable hangs from the ceiling. The cable has a density of $2 \text{ N/m}$. How much work is required to lift the entire cable to the ceiling i.e. so that none is hanging?
  2. A chain weighs $5$ pounds per foot and is initially lying coiled on the ground. How much work is needed to lift the chain so that it hangs vertically with its bottom touching the ground, fully extended to a height of $25 \text{ ft}$?
  3. How much work is needed to compress a spring ($k = 25 \text{ kg/s}^2$) $5.5 \text{ cm}$ from equilibrium?
  4. A rectangular trough is $2 \text{ feet}$ wide, $15 \text{ feet}$ long, and $2 \text{ feet}$ deep. It is filled with water, which weighs $62.4$ pound per cubic foot. How much work is required to pump all the water to the top of the trough?
  5. A tank is formed by rotating $y = \sqrt{400-x^2}$ on $[0,20]$ about the $y$-axis. The base is also fully enclosed, and the $x$ and $y$ axes are measured in meters. The tank is filled with diesel fuel of density $8240 \text{ N/m}^3$. How much work is needed to pump the liquid $10 \text{ meters}$ above the tank?
  6. How much work is needed to stretch a spring with $k = 5 \text{ lbs/ft}$ $15 \text{ cm}$ from its equilibrium position?
  7. A rope weighing $1/4 \text{ lb/ft}$ and totaling $16 \text{ feet}$ lies coiled on the ground. The midpoint ($8 \text{ feet}$ from each end) is lifted vertically until the two ends touch the ground, forming two fully extended $8 \text{ ft}$ segments. How much work was needed to lift the rope to this position?
  8. A $30 \text{ ft}$ chain hangs vertically from the ceiling and weighs $5$ pounds per foot. How much work is required to lift the bottom end of the chain up the ceiling, so that the chain is folded in half and both ends are hanging side-by-side from the ceiling, fully suspended?
  9. A tank is formed by rotating $x = \sqrt{100-(y-10)^2}$ on $[0,10]$ about the $y$-axis. The $x$ and $y$ axes are measured in meters. The tank is filled with fuel of density $8240 \text{ N/m}^3$. How much work is needed to pump the liquid to the top of the tank?
  10. A spring with $k = 500 \text{ kg/s}^2$ is initially compressed $2 \text{ cm}$ from equilibrium. How much work is needed to stretch the spring $5 \text{ cm}$ past equilibrium?
  11. Find the centroid of each region.
