Module 2

Boolean Connectives

Negation, conjunction, disjunction, and the syntax used to build compound sentences.

Guiding Questions

  • What do connectives do?
  • What does it mean when we say that connectives are truth-functional ?
  • What determines the truth value of a FOL sentence?
  • What is the Henkin-Hintikka game?
  • How is double negation different in natural language (e.g., English) and in FOL?
  • How do we avoid ambiguity in complex FOL formulas?

Objectives

  • Construct complex sentences using the three simplest connectives: conjunction, disjunction, and negation.
  • Use a truth table to determine the truth value of complex sentences constructed from atomic sentences and the connectives conjunction, disjunction, and negation.
  • Translate between colloquial English and complex sentences, constructed from conjunction, disjunction, and negation, of a general first-order language.
