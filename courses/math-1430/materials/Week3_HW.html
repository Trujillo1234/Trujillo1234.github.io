2. Radius & Endpoint Terms

For each power series below, perform the following steps:
  1. Find the Center ($a$).
  2. Use the Ratio (or Root) Test to find the Radius of Convergence ($R$).
  3. Identify the two Endpoints ($a-R$ and $a+R$).
  4. Plug in both endpoints and write out the first 4 terms of the resulting series.
(Note: You do not need to determine if the series converges at the endpoints yet.)
  1. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-2)^n}{n} $$
  2. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n (x+1)^n}{n \cdot 4^n} $$
  3. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} n! (x-5)^n $$
  4. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{2^n (x-3)^n}{n^2} $$
  5. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x+4)^n}{n \cdot 3^n} $$
  6. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{2n+1} $$
  7. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n(x-1)^n}{5^{n+1}} $$
  8. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-2)^n}{\sqrt{n}} $$
  9. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(2x)^n}{n} $$
  10. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (-1)^n \frac{(x+3)^n}{2^n} $$
