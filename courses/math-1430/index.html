Course Archive

MATH 1430: Calculus II, Spring 2026.

A current course archive organized around series, convergence, integration methods, applications of integration, work, centroids, arc length, surface area, and rational functions. The full weekly HTML archive is preserved, but this page gives the readable front door.

Open Full Weekly Archive

Series First

The course begins with Taylor polynomials, Taylor series, power series, radius and interval of convergence, and convergence tests.

Integration as Technique

The middle of the course develops substitution, integration by parts, trigonometric integrals, trigonometric substitution, and partial fractions.

Integration as Model

Applications include area, volume, arc length, surface area, work, pumping, center of mass, and centroids.
