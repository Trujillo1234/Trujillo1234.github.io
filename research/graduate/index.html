Graduate and Professional Research

Ramsey theory, ultrafilters, and foundations.

My professional research centers on topological Ramsey spaces, ultrafilters, Tukey theory, nonstandard arithmetic, and structural questions in mathematical foundations.

Research Documents

  • Doctoral dissertation: Topological Ramsey spaces, associated ultrafilters, and applications.
  • Master's thesis: Attractors for nonlinear reaction-diffusion equations in unbounded domains.

Research Areas

  • Abstract Ellentuck theorems and topological Ramsey spaces.
  • Selective and Ramsey ultrafilters on general Ramsey spaces.
  • Dedekind cuts of nonstandard arithmetic.
  • Connections among Ramsey theory, topology, and foundations.
