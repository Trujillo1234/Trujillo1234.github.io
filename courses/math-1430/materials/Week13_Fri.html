1. Polynomial Long Division

Polynomial Long Division Setup

$$ \begin{array}{r} D(x) \\ Q(x) \overline{ ) \ P(x)} \\ \vdots \quad \\ \hline R(x) \end{array} $$
$\hookrightarrow$ degree less than $Q$
$\longrightarrow$
$$ \frac{P(x)}{Q(x)} = D(x) + \frac{R(x)}{Q(x)} $$

2. Worked Examples

Example
$$ \int \frac{x^3 + 5x^2 + x + 1}{x - 3} \, dx $$
$$ \begin{array}{r} x^2 + 8x + 25 \\ x-3 \overline{ ) \ x^3 + 5x^2 + x + 1} \\ \underline{-(x^3 - 3x^2)} \phantom{{} + x + 1\ } \\ 8x^2 + x + 1 \\ \underline{-(8x^2 - 24x)} \phantom{{} + 1\ } \\ 25x + 1 \\ \underline{-(25x - 75)} \\ 76 \end{array} $$
Thus, $$ \int \frac{x^3 + 5x^2 + x + 1}{x - 3} \, dx = \int \left( x^2 + 8x + 25 + \frac{76}{x - 3} \right) dx $$
$$ = \frac{x^3}{3} + 4x^2 + 25x + 76\ln|x - 3| + C $$

Example
$$ \int \frac{10x^3 + 5x^2 + 3x - 7}{2x + 5} \, dx $$
$$ \begin{array}{r} 5x^2 - 10x + \frac{53}{2} \\ 2x+5 \overline{ ) \ 10x^3 + 5x^2 + 3x - 7} \\ \underline{-(10x^3 + 25x^2)} \phantom{{} + 3x - 7\ } \\ -20x^2 + 3x - 7 \\ \underline{-(-20x^2 - 50x)} \phantom{{} - 7\ } \\ 53x - 7 \\ \underline{-\left(53x + \frac{265}{2}\right)} \\ -\frac{279}{2} \end{array} $$
So, $$ = \int \left( 5x^2 - 10x + \frac{53}{2} - \frac{279/2}{2x + 5} \right) dx $$
$$ = \frac{5x^3}{3} - 5x^2 + \frac{53}{2}x - \frac{279}{4}\ln|2x + 5| + C $$
Example
$$ \int \frac{x^3 - 1}{x - 1} \, dx $$
$$ \begin{array}{r} x^2 + x + 1 \\ x-1 \overline{ ) \ x^3 + 0x^2 + 0x - 1} \\ \underline{-(x^3 - x^2)} \phantom{{} + 0x - 1\ } \\ x^2 + 0x - 1 \\ \underline{-(x^2 - x)} \phantom{{} - 1\ } \\ x - 1 \\ \underline{-(x - 1)} \\ 0 \end{array} $$
$$ \int \frac{x^3 - 1}{x - 1} \, dx = \int \left( x^2 + x + 1 + \frac{0}{x - 1} \right) dx $$
$$ = \frac{x^3}{3} + \frac{x^2}{2} + x + C $$
