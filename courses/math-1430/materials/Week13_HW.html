Calculus 2 • Rational Functions

Evaluate the Following Integrals

Evaluate the following indefinite integrals. Remember to include the constant of integration $+C$.

  1. $\displaystyle \int \frac{x^3-x^2+2x+3}{x+1}\,dx$
  2. $\displaystyle \int \frac{7x+1}{(x+1)(x-2)}\,dx$
  3. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x^2+9}\,dx$
  4. $\displaystyle \int \frac{x+7}{(x-1)(x+3)}\,dx$
  5. $\displaystyle \int \left(\frac{3}{(x+2)^2}+\frac{4}{x+2}\right)\,dx$
  6. $\displaystyle \int \arctan(x)\,dx$
  7. $\displaystyle \int \frac{x^3+2x}{x^2+1}\,dx$
  8. $\displaystyle \int \frac{x+8}{(x+1)(2x-3)}\,dx$
  9. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{4x^2+1}\,dx$
  10. $\displaystyle \int \frac{2x-4}{x(x-4)}\,dx$
  11. $\displaystyle \int \frac{2x^3+5x-1}{x-2}\,dx$
  12. $\displaystyle \int \frac{4x-1}{(x-1)(x+5)}\,dx$
  13. $\displaystyle \int \ln(x^2-1)\,dx$
  14. $\displaystyle \int \frac{5x-1}{(x-1)(3x+1)}\,dx$
  15. $\displaystyle \int \frac{5}{(2x+1)(x-2)}\,dx$
  16. $\displaystyle \int \frac{2x^2-x+4}{x^2+4}\,dx$
  17. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x^2+4x+5}\,dx$
  18. $\displaystyle \int \frac{x}{(x+2)(3x-1)}\,dx$
  19. $\displaystyle \int \frac{x^4+x^2-5}{x+2}\,dx$
  20. $\displaystyle \int \frac{3x+2}{(2x-1)(x+1)}\,dx$
