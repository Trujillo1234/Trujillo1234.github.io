MATH 1430.04 | Spring 2026

Calculus II

This page gathers the weekly materials for Calculus II: notes, classwork, homework, solutions, and diagrams. The course moves through power series, Taylor polynomials, convergence, integration techniques, applications of integration, differential equations, parametric and polar curves, and rational-function integration.

The schedule is preserved as a working course archive. Each week lists the class meetings, topic notes, and posted assignments or solutions where available.
