Modules 13/14

Completeness and Incompleteness

Deductive systems for first-order logic, completeness, incompleteness, and the limits of formal systems.

Guiding Questions

  • What does the completeness theorem say for first-order logic (FOL)?
  • What does the soundness theorem say for FOL?
  • What is the Henkin construction and how does it yield completeness?
  • What does Gödel's incompleteness theorem say about PA and ZF?
  • These will be our final modules on first-order logic . Here are some questions to consider as you complete the modules and explore their contents.

Objectives

  • State the soundness theorem for first-order logic (FOL).
  • State the completeness theorem for FOL.
  • State the incompleteness theorem for Peano Arithmetic (PA).

Reading and Video

  • Read the assigned textbook sections and watch the module lecture video. The public video embed will be added after the lecture files are uploaded to YouTube.

Assignments and Activities

  • Work through the module activities as practice with the formal language and proof tools.
