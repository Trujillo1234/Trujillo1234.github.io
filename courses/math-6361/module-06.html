Module 6

Soundness & Completeness

The relationship between proof and truth, soundness, completeness, and what a deductive system promises.

Guiding Questions

  • What does it mean when we say that a proof system is sound?
  • What does it mean when we say that a proof system is complete?
  • In deciding logical consequences, do truth tables and proofs always give us the same results?

Objectives

  • State the soundness theorem for propositional logic.
  • State the completeness theorem for propositional logic.
  • Recall that mathematical induction can be used to establish both the soundness and completeness theorems.
