1. Divergence Test

Apply the Divergence Test (a.k.a. the $n$th-term test) to each series. If the test is inconclusive, clearly state that it is inconclusive.
  1. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n+1}{2n+1} $$
  2. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{25}{n^2+1} $$
  3. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n^2+1}{n+1} $$
  4. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{\ln(n)}{n} $$
  5. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{n+1}{n} $$
  6. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n}{n^2} $$
  7. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{2}{n^2+n} $$
  8. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} 2^{n/3} $$
  9. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^n $$
  10. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} n $$
  11. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^5} $$
  12. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{5n^2+1}{n^2+n} $$

  3. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n}{n^2+n} $$

  7. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n (n+1)^2}{3n^2+1} $$
  8. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{5n}{n+2} $$

4. Center and Radius of Convergence

For each power series, identify the center and compute the radius of convergence. Use the Root Test where appropriate.
  1. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{n^2 x^n}{2^n} $$
  2. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n x^n}{\sqrt{n}} $$
  3. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{10^n (x-1)^n}{n!} $$
  4. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-5)^n}{n^n} $$

5. Derivatives and Integrals of Power Series

Differentiate and integrate each series term-by-term. State the center and radius of convergence for each resulting series.
  1. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} n x^n $$
  2. $$ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} 2^n x^n $$
  3. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{(2n)!} $$
  4. $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n (x-1)^n}{n} $$
