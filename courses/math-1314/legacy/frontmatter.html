MATH 1314: Precalculus Algebra

A PreTeXt course archive for precalculus algebra, maintained as a public teaching resource by Timothy Trujillo at Sam Houston State University. The course is organized by topic, with the table of contents on the left and embedded lecture videos inside the relevant sections.

Course Information

  • Course: MATH 1314, Precalculus Algebra.
  • Institution: Sam Houston State University.
  • Instructor archive: Timothy Trujillo.
  • Format: PreTeXt sections with embedded video lectures and sample assessments.

How to Read This Archive

Use the sidebar as the course table of contents. Each numbered section contains the original PreTeXt structure for that unit; lecture videos are embedded directly in the relevant subsections rather than separated into a second interface.

Course Directory

Embedded Videos

The video lectures remain inside the course sections. For example, the algebra videos begin in Equations and Inequalities, the function videos begin in Functions, and the exponential/logarithmic videos begin in Logarithms and Exponentials.

Assessment Materials

The assessment archive includes exam learning objectives, a sample midterm, a sample final, and recorded solution walkthroughs. Start with Exam Learning Objectives.
