Module 12

Axiomatic Set Theory

First-order axioms, set-theoretic language, separation, and mathematical structures.

Guiding Questions

  • What does the axiom of extension say about sets?
  • What does the axiom schema of separation say about sets?
  • What do the axioms of pair , union , and powerset say about sets?
  • What does the axiom of infinity say about sets?
  • What do the axioms of regularity and replacement say about sets?
  • What is a first-order structure ?
  • How are ordered pairs represented in set theory?

Objectives

  • Apply axiomatic set theory.
  • Apply basic set operations: power set , union , intersection , separation , and replacement .
  • Show that two sets are identical.
  • Show that one set is a subset of another set.
  • Construct first-order structures. Model relations and functions using set theory.
