1. Derivatives at a Point

Find the derivative $\frac{dy}{dx}$ evaluated at the specified values of $x$.

  1. $$ y = \sin(x) $$ at $x = \pi, \frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{3\pi}{4}$
  2. $$ y = \cos(x) $$ at $x = \pi, \frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{4}$
  3. $$ y = e^{2x} + e^{3x} $$ at $x = 0, 1$
  4. $$ y = x^2 + 2x + 1 $$ at $x = 1, 0, -1$
  5. $$ y = 5(x-7)^2 + 3(x-7) + 5 $$ at $x = 0, 7$
  6. $$ y = 1 + x + x^2 + x^3 + x^4 + \dots $$ at $x = 0, \frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{3}$
  7. $$ y = 1 + x^2 + x^4 + x^6 + \dots $$ at $x = 0, \frac{1}{10}$
  8. $$ y = 12(x-2)^2 + 5(x-2)^2 + \frac{1}{2}(x-2) + \frac{1}{3} $$ at $x = 2$
  9. $$ y = \ln(x^2 + 2x + 1) $$ at $x = 0$
  10. $$ y = \sqrt{x^2+x} $$ at $x = 2$

  1. $$ f(x) = e^{3x}, \quad a=0 $$
  2. $$ f(x) = \sin(5x), \quad a=\frac{\pi}{2} $$
  3. $$ g(x) = e^x + e^{-x}, \quad a=0 $$
  4. $$ g(x) = \frac{1}{1-x}, \quad a=0 $$

  6. $$ h(x) = \sqrt{x+1}, \quad a=3 $$

3. Calculus on Power Series

Find the derivative ($f'$) and the indefinite integral ($\int f(x) dx$) for each power series below.

  1. $$ f(x) = 1 + x + x^2 + x^3 + x^4 + \dots $$
  2. $$ g(x) = 1 + 2x^2 + 4x^4 + 6x^6 + \dots $$
  3. $$ h(x) = 1 - x + x^2 - x^3 + x^4 - \dots $$
  4. $$ i(x) = 5 + 5(x-2) + 5(x-2)^2 + 5(x-2)^3 + \dots $$
  5. $$ f(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n} $$
  6. $$ g(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{2^n} $$
  7. $$ h(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \left(-\frac{1}{2}\right)^n x^n $$
