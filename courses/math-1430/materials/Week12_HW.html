Homework 10

Calculus 2 • Trigonometric Integrals, Trig Substitution, Area, Centroids, and Arc Length

Evaluate the Following Integrals

  1. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{1+4x^2}\,dx$
  2. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{3+x^2}\,dx$
  3. $\displaystyle \int \frac{5}{\sqrt{x^2-1}}\,dx$
  4. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+6x+1}}\,dx$
  5. $\displaystyle \int \frac{5x}{\sqrt{x^2-1}}\,dx$
  6. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x^2-7}\,dx$
  7. $\displaystyle \int \sqrt{5+10x-x^2}\,dx$
  8. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{\sqrt{9-4x^2}}\,dx$
  9. $\displaystyle \int \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{x^2+4}}\,dx$
  10. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x\sqrt{x^2-16}}\,dx$
  11. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{9+x^2}\,dx$
  12. $\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{\sqrt{25+36x^2}}\,dx$
  13. $\displaystyle \int \sin^3(x)\cos^2(x)\,dx$
  14. $\displaystyle \int \sin^2(x)\cos^5(x)\,dx$
  15. $\displaystyle \int \pi \sin(x)\cos^5(x)\,dx$
  16. $\displaystyle \int \sec^2(x)\tan^3(x)\,dx$
  17. $\displaystyle \int \pi \sec^3(x)\tan(x)\,dx$
  18. $\displaystyle \int 7\tan^2(x)\sec(x)\,dx$
  19. $\displaystyle \int 8\tan^5(x)\sec^2(x)\,dx$
  20. $\displaystyle \int \sin(x)\cos^5(x)\,dx$

Applications

  1. Let $R$ be the region bounded above by the semicircle $y=\sqrt{9-x^2}$ and below by the $x$-axis over the interval $[-3,3]$.
    1. Find the area of the region.
    2. Compute the coordinates of the centroid $(\bar{x},\bar{y})$.
  2. Let $R$ be the region under the graph of $y=\dfrac{1}{x^2+4x+5}$, from $x=-2$ to $x=0$.
    1. Compute the area of the region.
    2. Find the coordinates of the centroid $(\bar{x},\bar{y})$.
  3. Find the arc length of the curve $y=x^2+4x+5$ on $[0,2]$.
  4. Find the arc length of the curve $y=-x^2+6x-5$ on $[2,4]$.
